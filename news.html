Man United's derby malaise, Milan teams dazzle, Xavi sees Barcelona's fragility, more

It seems like we say this every weekend, but this weekend's European soccer slate brought plenty of drama, joy, pain and talking points. Lionel Messi left Barcelona due to Financial issues

Lionel Messi is leaving Barcelona after a combination of the club's financial difficulties and La Liga's financial fair play rules meant a contract could not be finalised. It was understood that Messi would be renewing his contract at Camp Nou despite revealing in an exclusive interview with Goal last year of his intentions to leave.

Indian Hockey Team won Bronze medal

Indian men's hockey team displayed a great game of counter-attacking play as it won defeated Germany 5-4 in the bronze medal play-offs at the Tokyo Olympics today. Indian men's hockey team won an Olympic medal after a gap of 41 years. India's last of the eight Olympic golds came way back in the 1980 Moscow Games.
